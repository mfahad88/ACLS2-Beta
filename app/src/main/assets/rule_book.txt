Points will be awarded based on the following:
> Scoring 1 run = +1 point
> Scoring a 4 = +8 points
> Scoring a 6 = +12 points
> Scoring a 50 = +50 points
> Scoring a 100 = +100 points

> Scoring a Duck = -10 points
> Scoring a Golden Duck = -20 points
> On taking a catch = +10 points
> Wicketkeeper's 1 stump = +20 points

> Bowler Getting a wicket = +40 points
> Bowler Getting a Four wicket haul = +150 points
> Bowler Getting a Five Wicket Haul = +200 points
> Bowler getting a hat-trick = +300 points
> Bowling a maiden over = +50 points

> Captain x2 points (Captain's attained points are doubled)
> Vice-Captain x1.5 points (Vice-Captain's attained points are doubled)
